# Sample Queries for Agentic BI Dashboard

## Sales Analysis
1. "Show me sales by category"
2. "What's the sales trend over time?"
3. "Total sales"
4. "Show me product sales"

## ROI & Performance
5. "Show me ROI"
6. "Which channel has the best ROI?"
7. "ROI decomposition"
8. "Show me short-term vs long-term ROI"
9. "Return on investment for each channel"

## Contributions
10. "Show me contributions"
11. "What's the impact of each channel?"
12. "Which channel drives the most sales?"

## Correlations
13. "Show correlations"
14. "What's the relationship between channels?"
15. "Show me the correlation matrix"

## Brand Health
16. "Show me NPS"
17. "Brand analysis"
18. "How is our brand health?"
19. "NPS trend"

## Model Performance
20. "Model accuracy"
21. "Compare models"
22. "How accurate is the model?"
23. "Show model performance"

## Critique & Feedback
24. "Any issues with the model?"
25. "Show me critique feedback"
26. "What problems did you find?"

## General
27. "Help"
28. "What can you do?"

## Flexible Data Queries (NL2SQL)
29. "Show me the table rows in the data"
30. "What are the column names?"
31. "List all months where TV spend was above 50000"
32. "Rank channels by total contribution"
33. "What is the average sales?"

## Multi-Step Queries (Planning Agent)
34. "First show me sales trend, then tell me the best ROI channel"
35. "Compare model performance and then summarize the feedback"
36. "Analyze brand health and then show me correlations"
